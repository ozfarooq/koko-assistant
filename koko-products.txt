FABRIC CARE
----------------------------------------------------------------

Q: How do I wash my Koko Atelier outfit?
A: It depends on the fabric. Lawn and cambric can be machine washed cold with mild detergent. Karandi and khaddar should be dry cleaned or hand washed cold. Chiffon and organza must be dry cleaned only and stored folded. Embroidered pieces should always be dry cleaned and stored in dust bags.

Q: Can I machine wash my outfit?
A: Only lawn and cambric pieces can be machine washed on cold. All other fabrics should be dry cleaned.

Q: How should I store my outfit?
A: Chiffon and organza should be stored folded, not hung. Embroidered pieces should be stored in dust bags to protect the work.

Q: Can I bleach my clothes?
A: No, do not bleach any Koko Atelier garments as it will damage the fabric and colour.
